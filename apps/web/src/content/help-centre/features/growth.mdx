## What Is the AI Growth Advisor?

The AI Growth Advisor is Tutorwise's built-in business intelligence assistant — a conversational AI that helps every type of user on the platform grow their income, sharpen their strategy, and make better business decisions. It's available 24/7 from your dashboard with a free tier (10 questions/day) and a Growth Pro upgrade (£10/month for 5,000 questions/month).

- **Analyses** your current income streams and identifies gaps
- **Optimises** your listings, pricing, and profile visibility
- **Builds** referral pipelines and calculates commission potential
- **Advises** on passive income through AI Tutors and marketplace listings
- **Guides** on compliance, business setup, and platform best practices

### Who Is the Growth Advisor For?

| Role | How Growth Helps |
|------|----------------|
| **Tutors** | Pricing strategy, listing optimisation, income diversification, AI Tutor creation |
| **Agents** | Referral pipeline building, commission maximisation, recruitment strategy |
| **Clients** | Earning passively through referrals, understanding AI Tutor options |
| **Organisations** | Growing margin, recruiting tutors, expanding service offerings |

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Growth vs Lexi:** The Growth Advisor is your **business strategist** — it focuses on income, pricing, referrals, and business growth. [Lexi](/help-centre/features/lexi) is your **platform assistant** — it helps with bookings, payments, and navigating Tutorwise. If you ask Lexi a business strategy question, it will suggest Growth automatically.
</CalloutBox>

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific about your situation.** "I charge £45/hr for GCSE Maths in Manchester" gets better advice than "I'm a tutor"
2. **Share your numbers.** The more context you give (current rate, number of students, weekly hours), the more accurate the advice
3. **Ask for action plans.** "Give me a 30-day plan to increase my income by 20%" gets you step-by-step guidance
4. **Explore income diversification.** Ask the advisor to list all ways you could earn on Tutorwise — you may be missing streams
5. **Use suggestion chips.** After each response, tap the chips to drill deeper into any point
6. **Return regularly.** Business strategy is iterative — use Growth for ongoing decisions, not just one-off questions

## Troubleshooting

**Q: The Growth Advisor seems to be giving generic advice. How do I get more personalised responses?**
A: Include more context in your question — your current income, role, location, and specific goals. The more specific you are, the more tailored the advice.

**Q: I'm a tutor but the advisor is giving me agent-focused advice. Is something wrong?**
A: Your role is detected from your active profile. If you have multiple roles, check your profile settings to confirm your active role, then start a new session.

**Q: Can the Growth Advisor see my actual earnings data?**
A: Not currently — the advisor uses your profile information and any context you share in the conversation. For detailed earnings analysis, visit your [Financials](/financials) page and share specific numbers with the advisor.

**Q: How is the Growth Advisor different from asking Lexi about income?**
A: [Lexi](/help-centre/features/lexi) handles platform operations — bookings, payments, and navigation. The Growth Advisor is a dedicated business strategist with deep knowledge of income optimisation, market positioning, and referral strategy. Lexi will direct you to Growth for business questions.

**Q: Is my conversation history saved?**
A: Yes. All sessions are saved and viewable from [Growth History](/growth/history). Sessions expire after 24 hours, but completed conversations remain in your history.

**Q: Can anyone else see my Growth conversations?**
A: No. Your conversations are private to you.
