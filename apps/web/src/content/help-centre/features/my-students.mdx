## Adding Session Notes

**During or After Sessions:**
1. Go to student's session history
2. Click "Add Note" on specific session
3. Write notes about:
   - Topics covered today
   - Student's understanding level
   - Homework assigned
   - Next session focus
   - Parent/student concerns

**Why Take Notes:**
- Remember previous sessions
- Track progress over time
- Personalize future lessons
- Show professionalism
- Handle disputes with records

**Note Privacy:**
- Only you can see your notes
- Not visible to student or platform
- Stored permanently
- Exportable for your records

## Re-engaging Inactive Students

**When to Reach Out:**
- 30 days since last session (gentle check-in)
- 60 days (offer special discount or package)
- 90 days (re-introduction, ask if they need help)

**Message Templates:**

**30-Day Check-In:**
> "Hi [Name]! Hope you're doing well! Just checking in - how are you finding [Subject] since our last session? Let me know if you'd like to schedule a follow-up!"

**60-Day Re-Engagement:**
> "Hi [Name]! I have some availability opening up and thought of you. Would you like to book a session? Happy to offer 20% off as a welcome-back bonus!"

**90-Day Reconnection:**
> "Hi [Name]! It's been a while! I hope everything's going well with your studies. If you ever need help with [Subject] again, I'm here. Best of luck!"

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Why don't I see all my students?**
A: Check your filters. Students only appear after completing at least one paid session with you.

**Q: How do I remove a student from my list?**
A: You can't remove students, but you can archive them to hide from default view.

**Q: Can students see the notes I write about them?**
A: No, session notes are completely private and only visible to you.

**Q: How do I export my student list?**
A: Click "Export" button in My Students dashboard. Downloads CSV with all data.

**Q: What if a student books with multiple tutors?**
A: They'll appear in each tutor's My Students list independently. No cross-tutor visibility.
