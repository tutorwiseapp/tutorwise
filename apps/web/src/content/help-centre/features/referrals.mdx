# Referral System Guide

Earn commissions by referring tutors, students, and agents to Tutorwise. Our multi-tier referral system rewards you for building the network.

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Earn 10% commission** on every booking made by your referrals for the first 12 months.
</CalloutBox>

## How Referrals Work

### Three Types of Referrals

1. **Tutor Referrals**: Earn from their bookings
2. **Student Referrals**: Earn from their purchases
3. **Agent Referrals**: Earn from their network activity

### Commission Structure

<Tabs>
  <Tab label="Tutor Referrals">
    **When you refer a tutor:**

    - **10% commission** on all their bookings
    - **Duration**: First 12 months
    - **Minimum**: No minimum booking amount
    - **Maximum**: Unlimited earnings

    **Example:**
    - Tutor completes £100 session
    - Platform fee: £15
    - Tutor receives: £85
    - **You receive: £10** (10% of £100)
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Student Referrals">
    **When you refer a student:**

    - **5% commission** on their bookings
    - **Duration**: First 6 months
    - **Applies to**: All sessions they book

    **Example:**
    - Student books £50 session
    - **You receive: £2.50** (5% of £50)
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Agent Referrals">
    **When you refer an agent:**

    - **3% commission** on their network
    - **Duration**: First 12 months
    - **Includes**: All bookings in their network

    **Example:**
    - Agent's network generates £1,000
    - **You receive: £30** (3% of £1,000)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Referral Delegation

Supercharge your referral network by delegating to agents.

### What is Delegation?

- Assign part of your commission to agents
- They promote on your behalf
- Split commissions automatically
- Build a referral network

### How to Delegate

1. Go to [Referrals](/referrals) → Delegation
2. Search for agent by referral code
3. Set delegation percentage (10-50%)
4. Confirm delegation
5. Agent starts promoting

**Example:**
- You earn 10% on tutor bookings
- Delegate 30% to an agent
- Agent earns 3% (30% of 10%)
- You still earn 7%
- Agent motivates active promotion

### Finding Good Delegates

**Look for agents who:**
- Have large networks
- Are active on social media
- Create educational content
- Have marketing experience

## Common Questions

**How long do I earn commissions?**
- Tutor referrals: 12 months
- Student referrals: 6 months
- Agent referrals: 12 months

**Can I refer myself?**
No, self-referrals are not permitted.

**What happens after the commission period?**
- Commission stops after expiry
- You keep all earned commissions
- Can refer the same person again if they leave and rejoin

**Can I see who I've referred?**
Yes, view all referrals in your dashboard (privacy-protected).

**Do referrals need to use my link?**
Yes, they must sign up through your unique referral link.

**What if someone uses multiple referral codes?**
First code used gets the commission.
