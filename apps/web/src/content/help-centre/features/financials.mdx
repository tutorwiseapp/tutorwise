## Understanding Your Earnings

### Types of Income

<Tabs>
  <Tab label="Session Earnings">
    **Direct Teaching Income:**

    From completed bookings where you're the tutor:
    - Hourly rate × session duration

    - Net earnings added to balance

    ```
    Session: 2 hours @ £50/hour = £100

    ```

    - Session completes → Payment processes (24hrs)
    - Added to pending balance
    - Clearing period (7 days)
    - Moves to available balance
    - Weekly payout → Bank account
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Referral Commissions">

    - 10% **lifetime** commission on ALL their future bookings

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Tax Time:** Use filters to export transactions for specific date ranges. Perfect for tax reporting and accounting!
</CalloutBox>

### Transaction Statuses

**Pending:**
- Session completed but in clearing period
- Usually 7 days for new tutors
- 3 days for established tutors (50+ sessions)
- Protection against chargebacks

**Cleared:**
- Clearing period passed
- Added to available balance
- Ready for next payout
- No longer reversible

**Paid Out:**
- Included in weekly payout
- Transferred to your bank
- Receipt available
- Shows payout batch ID

**Refunded:**
- Client requested refund (within policy)
- Amount reversed from earnings
- Shows original transaction link
- Explanation provided
- Yellow warning banner displayed

**Disputed (New!):**
- Payment disputed by payer (chargeback)
- Funds held pending investigation
- Red alert banner with prominent warning
- Link to Stripe Dashboard for evidence submission
- Shows dispute status and reason
- 7-14 day resolution period

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Dispute Alert:** If you see a red "Payment Disputed" banner on a transaction:
1. Click "View in Stripe Dashboard" immediately
2. Upload evidence (session recordings, messages, completion proof)
3. Respond within 7 days for best chance of winning
4. Contact support@tutorwise.com if you need help
</CalloutBox>

## Managing Your Balance

### Available Balance

**What Is It?**
- Money cleared and ready to withdraw
- Not yet paid out to bank
- Accumulates until next payout

**Minimum Payout:**
- £20 minimum for tutoring earnings
- £25 minimum for referral commissions
- Below threshold: Carries to next week automatically
- No maximum limit

### Upcoming Earnings Projections (New!)

**Track Future Earnings:**
Your Wallet Balance Widget now shows upcoming earnings automatically:
- **Expandable View**: Click "Upcoming Earnings" to see when clearing funds become available
- **Grouped by Date**: See exactly which dates your money will clear
- **Amount Breakdown**: Shows total amount clearing on each date
- **7-Day Timeline**: Funds become available 7 days after session completion

**Example:**
```
Upcoming Earnings:
14 Feb 2026: £45.00  (2 transactions)
17 Feb 2026: £90.00  (1 transaction)
20 Feb 2026: £150.00 (3 transactions)
```

This helps you:
- Plan cash flow
- Know when to expect payments
- Track multiple sessions at once
- Avoid surprises

<CalloutBox type="success">
**Pro Tip:** Check "Upcoming Earnings" before making financial plans. You'll know exactly when each payment becomes available!
</CalloutBox>

### Payout Schedule (Automated System)

**Weekly Automatic Payouts:**
- Every Friday at 10:00 AM UTC (automated batch processor)
- Includes all cleared transactions (teaching earnings + referral commissions)
- Transferred via Stripe Connect to your bank
- Arrives in 2-5 business days

**Minimum Thresholds:**
- **Tutoring earnings:** £20 minimum for payout
- **Referral commissions:** £25 minimum for automated payout
- Below threshold: Carries to next week automatically

**Tracking Payouts:**
- Email notification when payout is processed
- Email notification if payout fails (update bank details)
- Reference ID for bank matching
- View all payouts in Financials dashboard

<CalloutBox type="info">
**First Payout:** May take 7-10 days for initial Stripe verification. Subsequent payouts are fully automated every Friday.
</CalloutBox>

### Withdrawals & Bank Details

**Update Bank Account:**
1. Go to [Account](/account) → Payment Settings
2. Manage Stripe account
3. Update bank details
4. Verify new account (micro-deposits)

**Manual Withdrawal (New!):**
You can now request withdrawals directly from the Financials page:
1. Go to [Financials](/financials)
2. Click "Request Withdrawal" button (top right)
3. Review available balance and processing time (2-3 business days)
4. Confirm withdrawal
5. Funds transferred to your Stripe Connect account
6. You'll receive email confirmation

**Withdrawal Limitations:**
- Only one withdrawal request can be active at a time
- Must wait 15 minutes between withdrawal requests
- Available balance only (clearing funds not eligible)
- Cannot withdraw if previous withdrawal is processing

**Withdrawal History:**
- View all past withdrawals in the new "Withdrawal History" section
- Track status: Processing, Completed, Failed, Disputed
- See estimated arrival dates
- Link to Stripe Dashboard for each payout

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Flexible Options:** You can request manual withdrawals at any time OR wait for the automated Friday payouts. Choose what works best for your cash flow!
</CalloutBox>

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Why is my balance different from what I expected?**
A: Check for:
- Platform fees (10% deducted)
- Pending transactions (not yet cleared)
- Recent refunds
- Previous payouts already sent

**Q: When will my pending payment clear?**
A: New tutors: 7 days. Established tutors (50+ sessions): 3 days. Check transaction status for exact date.

**Q: I didn't receive my Friday payout. Where is it?**
A: Check:
- Email for payout notification (sent when processed)
- Email for payout failed notification (if bank details issue)
- Stripe dashboard for transfer status
- Bank account (may take 2-5 business days to arrive)
- Minimum balance requirement (£20 teaching, £25 referral commissions)

**Q: How do I dispute a refund?**
A: Email support@tutorwise.com with:
- Transaction ID
- Explanation of dispute
- Supporting evidence
We review within 48 hours.

**Q: Can I change my payout frequency?**
A: Not currently. Weekly Friday payouts are standard for all users.

**Q: Why was a transaction reversed?**
A: Possible reasons:
- Client disputed charge
- Payment method failed
- Violation of terms
- Administrative error
Check transaction notes for details.

**Q: How do I request a manual withdrawal?**
A: Click "Request Withdrawal" on the [Financials](/financials) page. You can only withdraw your available balance (not clearing funds). One withdrawal can be processed at a time.

**Q: Why can't I request another withdrawal?**
A: You may have:
- A withdrawal already processing (wait 15 minutes)
- No available balance (only cleared funds can be withdrawn)
- A previous withdrawal that failed (check email)
- Need to update bank details in Stripe

**Q: What does the "Upcoming Earnings" section show?**
A: It shows when your clearing funds will become available. Click to expand and see grouped earnings by date. Funds clear 7 days after session completion.

**Q: What should I do if my transaction shows a dispute banner?**
A: Act immediately:
1. Click "View in Stripe Dashboard"
2. Review dispute reason
3. Upload evidence (session proof, messages, recordings)
4. Respond within 7 days
5. Contact support if you need assistance
