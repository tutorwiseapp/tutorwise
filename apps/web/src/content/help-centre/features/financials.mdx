## Understanding Your Earnings

### Types of Income

<Tabs>
  <Tab label="Session Earnings">
    **Direct Teaching Income:**

    From completed bookings where you're the tutor:
    - Hourly rate × session duration
    - Platform fee deducted (15%)
    - Net earnings added to balance

    **Example:**
    ```
    Session: 2 hours @ £50/hour = £100
    Platform Fee (15%): -£15
    Your Earnings: £85
    ```

    **Payment Timeline:**
    - Session completes → Payment processes (24hrs)
    - Added to pending balance
    - Clearing period (7 days)
    - Moves to available balance
    - Weekly payout → Bank account
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Referral Commissions">
    **From Referrals System:**

    When users you referred complete sessions:
    - 10% commission on tutor's earnings
    - Tracked automatically
    - Shows referral source

    **Example:**
    ```
    Referred Tutor Earns: £85
    Your Commission (10%): £8.50
    ```

    See [Referrals Guide](/help-centre/features/referrals) for details.
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Wiselist Commissions">
    **From Shared Wiselists:**

    When someone books via your public wiselist:
    - 5% commission on booking value
    - Passive income potential
    - Multiple attribution sources

    **Example:**
    ```
    Booking from Your List: £100
    Your Commission (5%): £5
    ```

    See [Wiselists Guide](/help-centre/features/wiselists) for details.
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Organisation Revenue">
    **For Organisation Owners:**

    Aggregate team earnings:
    - All member sessions combined
    - Your individual earnings
    - Commission from team referrals
    - Performance analytics

    See [Organisations Guide](/help-centre/features/organisations) for details.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Transaction Details

### Viewing Individual Transactions

Each transaction shows:

**Transaction Information:**
- Date and time
- Transaction type (Session, Referral, Wiselist, Refund)
- Amount (gross and net)
- Platform fee breakdown
- Payment status (Pending, Cleared, Paid Out)

**Session Context:**
- Client name
- Subject taught
- Session duration
- Listing used
- Location (online/in-person)

**Filtering & Search:**
- Date range picker
- Transaction type
- Payment status
- Client or service name
- Amount range

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Tax Time:** Use filters to export transactions for specific date ranges. Perfect for tax reporting and accounting!
</CalloutBox>

### Transaction Statuses

**Pending:**
- Session completed but in clearing period
- Usually 7 days for new tutors
- 3 days for established tutors (50+ sessions)
- Protection against chargebacks

**Cleared:**
- Clearing period passed
- Added to available balance
- Ready for next payout
- No longer reversible

**Paid Out:**
- Included in weekly payout
- Transferred to your bank
- Receipt available
- Shows payout batch ID

**Refunded:**
- Client requested refund (within policy)
- Amount reversed from earnings
- Shows original transaction link
- Explanation provided

## Managing Your Balance

### Available Balance

**What Is It?**
- Money cleared and ready to withdraw
- Not yet paid out to bank
- Accumulates until next payout

**Minimum Payout:**
- £20 minimum for automatic payout
- Below £20: Carries to next week
- No maximum limit

### Payout Schedule

**Weekly Payouts:**
- Every Friday at 12:00 PM GMT
- Includes all cleared transactions
- Transferred via Stripe to bank
- Arrives in 2-5 business days

**Tracking Payouts:**
- Email notification when sent
- Reference ID for bank matching
- Detailed breakdown PDF
- Transaction list included

<CalloutBox type="info">
**First Payout:** May take 7-10 days for initial verification. Subsequent payouts are faster (2-5 days).
</CalloutBox>

### Withdrawals & Bank Details

**Update Bank Account:**
1. Go to [Account](/account) → Payment Settings
2. Manage Stripe account
3. Update bank details
4. Verify new account (micro-deposits)

**Manual Withdrawal:**
- Not currently available
- Weekly automatic payouts only
- Contact support for emergencies

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Why is my balance different from what I expected?**
A: Check for:
- Platform fees (15% deducted)
- Pending transactions (not yet cleared)
- Recent refunds
- Previous payouts already sent

**Q: When will my pending payment clear?**
A: New tutors: 7 days. Established tutors (50+ sessions): 3 days. Check transaction status for exact date.

**Q: I didn't receive my Friday payout. Where is it?**
A: Check:
- Email for payout notification
- Stripe dashboard for transfer status
- Bank account (may take 2-5 days)
- Minimum £20 balance requirement

**Q: How do I dispute a refund?**
A: Email support@tutorwise.com with:
- Transaction ID
- Explanation of dispute
- Supporting evidence
We review within 48 hours.

**Q: Can I change my payout frequency?**
A: Not currently. Weekly Friday payouts are standard for all users.

**Q: Why was a transaction reversed?**
A: Possible reasons:
- Client disputed charge
- Payment method failed
- Violation of terms
- Administrative error
Check transaction notes for details.
