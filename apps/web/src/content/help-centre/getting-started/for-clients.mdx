## What is a Client on Tutorwise?

Clients are parents, guardians, or individuals who book tutoring sessions for students. As a client, you can:

- Manage multiple students under one account
- Find and book qualified tutors
- Track session history and progress
- Handle payments and invoices
- Communicate with tutors
- Earn referral commissions

## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

### Account Information
Set up your client account:
- Your name and contact details
- Profile photo (optional)
- Preferred communication method
- Billing address for invoices

### Add Your Students

Add one or more students to your account:
1. Go to **My Students**
2. Click **Add Student**
3. Enter student details:
   - Name and age
   - Current grade level
   - Subjects of interest
   - Learning goals
   - Any special requirements

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Multi-Student Management**: You can add unlimited students to your account and book different tutors for each one.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 2: Find the Right Tutor

Use our [Marketplace](/marketplace) to search for qualified tutors.

### Search and Filter

Filter tutors by:
- **Subject**: Maths, English, Science, Languages, etc.
- **Level**: Primary, GCSE, A-Level, University
- **Session Type**: Online, In-person, or Hybrid
- **Price Range**: £15-£100+ per hour
- **Availability**: Match your student's schedule
- **Location**: For in-person sessions
- **Qualifications**: Degrees, certifications, DBS checks

### Evaluate Tutor Profiles

Look for:
- **Qualifications**: Verified degrees and certifications
- **Experience**: Years teaching and student success stories
- **Reviews**: Ratings from other parents and students
- **Teaching Style**: Approach and methodology
- **Availability**: Flexible scheduling options
- **Pricing**: Clear hourly rates and package deals

<CalloutBox type="success">
**Quality Assurance**: All tutors on Tutorwise are vetted and verified. Look for the verified badge for added confidence.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 3: Book Your First Session

### Booking Process

1. **Select Student**
   - Choose which student the session is for
   - Tutors will see student's grade and subject needs

2. **Choose Time Slot**
   - Browse tutor's available times
   - Select duration (30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours)
   - Pick recurring sessions if needed

3. **Add Session Details**
   - Specify topic or curriculum area
   - Share homework or assessment materials
   - Mention specific goals or challenges
   - Add any special instructions

4. **Review and Confirm**
   - Double-check all details
   - Agree to cancellation policy
   - Complete booking

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Flexible Cancellation**: Cancel up to 24 hours before the session for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur charges.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 4: Set Up Payments

### Add Payment Method

Before booking, add a payment method:

1. Go to **Account** → **Payments**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details securely
4. Set as default (optional)
5. Save for future bookings

### Payment Process

**How Payments Work:**
- Payment authorized when booking
- Held securely until session completes
- Released to tutor 24 hours after session
- Refunded automatically if tutor cancels

**Session Pricing:**
- Set by individual tutors
- Charged per hour or session
- Discounts for package bookings
- No platform booking fees

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Payment Security**: All payments processed through Stripe. Your card details are never stored on Tutorwise servers.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 5: Manage Sessions

### Before the Session

**Preparation Checklist:**
- Confirm session time with student
- Ensure device and internet work (for online)
- Prepare materials or homework
- Set up study space
- Remind student of session goals

### During the Session

**Your Role:**
- Let tutor and student work together
- Be available if needed
- Avoid interrupting unless necessary
- Take notes on progress (optional)

**For Young Students:**
- Supervise from nearby location
- Assist with technical issues
- Ensure student stays engaged

### After the Session

**Post-Session Actions:**
1. Review session summary from tutor
2. Check student understanding
3. Rate and review the tutor
4. Book next session if satisfied
5. Address any concerns with tutor

## Common Questions

**Can I switch tutors if not satisfied?**
Yes, you can book different tutors anytime. We recommend trying 2-3 sessions before deciding.

**What if my student needs help with multiple subjects?**
Book different tutors for each subject, all managed under your account.

**Are there contracts or commitments?**
No contracts required. Book sessions as needed with complete flexibility.

**What if the tutor doesn't show up?**
Contact support immediately. You'll receive a full refund and credit toward your next session.

**Can I get a refund after a session?**
Refunds are case-by-case. If you're unsatisfied, contact the tutor first, then support if unresolved.

**Do tutors have DBS checks?**
Many tutors have DBS clearance. Look for the DBS verified badge on profiles and ask tutors directly.

## Safety and Safeguarding

### Protecting Your Student

**Safety Guidelines:**
- Always supervise first meetings
- Verify tutor identity and credentials
- Keep sessions in supervised areas
- Use platform messaging (tracked and secure)
- Report any concerns immediately

**For In-Person Sessions:**
- Meet in public location first time
- Ensure other adults are home
- Check tutor has valid DBS
- Trust your instincts

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Report Issues**: If you have any safety concerns, contact our safeguarding team immediately at safeguarding@tutorwise.com
</CalloutBox>
