# Getting Started for Organisations

Welcome to Tutorwise! This guide will help you set up your organisation account and manage institutional tutoring programs at scale.

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Educational Institution?** Whether you're a school, tutoring center, or educational charity, Tutorwise provides enterprise-level tools for managing tutoring at scale.
</CalloutBox>

## What is an Organisation on Tutorwise?

Organisations are schools, tutoring centers, educational charities, or companies that:

- Manage tutoring programs for multiple students
- Employ or contract multiple tutors
- Need bulk booking and billing
- Require administrative oversight
- Want detailed analytics and reporting
- Need compliance and safeguarding tools

### Organisation Types

**We Support:**
- Primary and secondary schools
- Universities and colleges
- Private tutoring centers
- Educational charities
- Corporate training providers
- Home education cooperatives
- Special educational needs providers

## Step 1: Organisation Setup

### Create Organisation Account

1. Sign up with organisational email
2. Select **Organisation** account type
3. Complete organisation verification:
   - Organisation name and registration number
   - Business address
   - Contact details
   - Tax information (if applicable)

### Organisation Profile

**Required Information:**
- Official organisation name
- Logo and branding
- Type of institution
- Number of students
- Service area and locations
- Subjects offered
- Accreditations and certifications

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Verification Process**: Organisation accounts undergo enhanced verification (1-3 business days) to ensure authenticity and safeguarding compliance.
</CalloutBox>

### Add Administrators

Set up your admin team:

1. Go to **Organisation** → **Team**
2. Add administrators by email
3. Assign roles and permissions:
   - **Super Admin**: Full access
   - **Tutor Manager**: Recruit and manage tutors
   - **Student Manager**: Manage bookings and students
   - **Finance**: Billing and payments only
   - **Viewer**: Read-only access

## Step 2: Build Your Tutor Team

### Recruiting Tutors

**Internal Tutors (Employees):**
- Add tutors directly to organisation
- Set employment status
- Manage contracts and compliance
- Control pricing and availability

**External Tutors (Contractors):**
- Invite independent tutors to your network
- Set commission agreements
- Maintain quality standards
- Flexible engagement terms

### Adding Tutors

1. Go to **Organisation** → **Tutors**
2. Click **Add Tutor**
3. Choose method:
   - **Invite by Email**: Send invitation link
   - **Bulk Upload**: CSV import for multiple tutors
   - **Direct Add**: Create profile on their behalf

### Tutor Onboarding

**Onboarding Checklist:**
- Profile creation and verification
- DBS check upload and verification
- Qualification verification
- Contract and compliance documents
- Training and platform orientation
- Subject and availability setup

<CalloutBox type="success">
**Streamlined Onboarding**: Our bulk onboarding tools can process 50+ tutors in under an hour.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 3: Student Management

### Adding Students

**Single Student:**
1. Go to **Students** → **Add Student**
2. Enter student details
3. Assign to classes or groups
4. Set learning objectives

**Bulk Import:**
1. Download CSV template
2. Fill in student details
3. Upload to platform
4. Review and confirm

### Student Groups

**Organise by:**
- Year groups or grades
- Subject classes
- Intervention groups
- Special educational needs
- Ability levels

### Assigning Students to Tutors

**Matching Options:**
- Manual assignment by admin
- Student/parent choice from approved list
- Automated matching based on needs
- Group assignments for classes

## Step 4: Booking and Scheduling

### Bulk Booking

Create sessions for multiple students:

1. Select student group
2. Choose tutor(s)
3. Set recurring schedule
4. Define session parameters:
   - Subject and topic
   - Duration and frequency
   - Start and end dates
   - Location (online/in-person)

### Scheduling Tools

**Features:**
- Calendar view for all sessions
- Resource conflict detection
- Room booking (for in-person)
- Automated reminders
- Waitlist management

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Smart Scheduling**: Set up recurring sessions once and let the system handle scheduling, reminders, and tutor assignments.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 5: Payment and Billing

### Payment Models

**Choose Your Model:**

1. **Centralized Billing**
   - Organisation pays for all sessions
   - Students book freely from approved tutors
   - Monthly consolidated invoice
   - Budget controls available

2. **Individual Billing**
   - Parents/students pay directly
   - Organisation sets approved tutor list
   - No financial liability
   - Commission on bookings (optional)

3. **Hybrid Model**
   - Organisation subsidises sessions
   - Students pay difference
   - Flexible funding options
   - Track subsidies in real-time

### Budget Management

**Control Spending:**
- Set budget limits per student/group
- Approval workflows for bookings
- Spending alerts and notifications
- Real-time budget tracking
- Forecasting tools

### Invoicing

**Automated Invoicing:**
- Monthly consolidated invoices
- Breakdown by tutor, student, subject
- CSV/PDF export
- Integration with accounting systems
- Purchase order support

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Payment Terms**: Organisations typically operate on NET 30 payment terms. Contact us for custom arrangements.
</CalloutBox>
