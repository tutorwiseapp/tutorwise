# Getting Started for Students

Welcome to Tutorwise! This guide will help you find the perfect tutor and book your first session.

<CalloutBox type="info">
**New to Tutorwise?** Finding and booking a tutor takes just a few minutes!
</CalloutBox>

## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

A complete profile helps tutors understand your learning needs.

### Basic Information
- Add your name and profile photo
- Select your grade level or education stage
- List subjects you're interested in learning

### Learning Goals
Tell tutors what you want to achieve:
- Exam preparation (GCSEs, A-Levels, SATs)
- Homework help
- Skill building
- Test prep

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Tip**: The more specific you are about your goals, the better tutors can tailor their teaching approach.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 2: Find Your Perfect Tutor

Use our [Marketplace](/marketplace) to search for tutors.

### Search Filters

Filter tutors by:
- **Subject**: Mathematics, English, Science, Languages, etc.
- **Level**: Primary, GCSE, A-Level, University
- **Session Type**: Online, In-person, or both
- **Price Range**: Set your budget
- **Availability**: When you need sessions

### Review Tutor Profiles

Look for:
- Verified qualifications and experience
- Student reviews and ratings
- Clear pricing per hour
- Available time slots
- Teaching specialties

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Pro Tip**: Read reviews from other students to understand the tutor's teaching style and effectiveness.
</CalloutBox>

## Step 3: Book Your First Session

Found a tutor you like? Here's how to book:

### Booking Process

1. **Select a Time Slot**
   - Browse tutor's available times
   - Choose duration (30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours)
   - Pick a date that works for you

2. **Add Session Details**
   - Specify the topic or subject
   - Share any materials or homework
   - Mention specific goals for the session

3. **Add Payment Method**
   - Securely save your payment card
   - Payment is only charged after session completion
   - Cancel for free up to 24 hours before

4. **Confirm Booking**
   - Review session details
   - Confirm and receive booking confirmation
   - Add to your calendar

<Tabs>
  <Tab label="Online Sessions">
    For online sessions:
    - Check your internet connection
    - Test your camera and microphone
    - Join via our video platform (no downloads needed)
    - Be ready 5 minutes early
  </Tab>
  <Tab label="In-Person Sessions">
    For in-person sessions:
    - Confirm the meeting location
    - Arrive on time with materials
    - Bring notebooks and relevant textbooks
    - Ensure you have a quiet study space
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

[Learn more about how bookings work →](/help-centre/features/bookings)

## Step 4: Prepare for Your Session

Make the most of your tutoring session!

### Before the Session

- **Review Materials**: Look over topics you need help with
- **List Questions**: Write down specific questions
- **Gather Resources**: Have textbooks, notes, or homework ready
- **Test Tech**: For online sessions, test your setup

### During the Session

- **Be Engaged**: Ask questions and participate actively
- **Take Notes**: Write down key concepts and tips
- **Be Honest**: Tell your tutor if you don't understand something
- **Track Time**: Stay aware of session duration

### After the Session

- **Review Notes**: Go over what you learned
- **Practice**: Complete any homework or exercises
- **Leave Feedback**: Rate your tutor and leave a review
- **Book Next Session**: Schedule follow-up if helpful

<CalloutBox type="success">
**Success Tip**: Regular sessions (1-2 times per week) lead to better learning outcomes than sporadic tutoring.
</CalloutBox>

## Payment Information

### How Payments Work

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Secure Payments**: All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We never store your full card details.
</CalloutBox>

- **When Charged**: After session completion (within 24 hours)
- **Payment Methods**: Credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Refunds**: Available for cancellations and issues
- **Receipts**: Automatic email receipts for all payments

### Pricing
- Tutors set their own hourly rates
- Rates typically range from £15-50/hour
- Package deals often available for bulk bookings
- No hidden fees or platform charges to students
