## Why Stripe?

Stripe is our payment processor because it's:
- **Secure**: Industry-leading encryption and fraud protection
- **Fast**: Payouts in 2-5 business days
- **Reliable**: 99.99% uptime
- **Global**: Supports multiple currencies and countries

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:
- Government-issued ID (passport or driver's license)
- Bank account details
- Business information (if applicable)
- Tax ID or National Insurance number

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Important**: You must be 18+ to create a Stripe account and receive payments.
</CalloutBox>

## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Navigate to Payments

1. Log in to your Tutorwise account
2. Go to Account → Professional Info
3. Scroll to "Payment Settings"
4. Click "Connect Stripe Account"

### Step 2: Choose Account Type

**New to Stripe?**
- Click "Create new Stripe account"
- Follow Stripe's onboarding flow
- You'll be redirected back to Tutorwise when complete

**Already have Stripe?**
- Click "Connect existing account"
- Log in to your Stripe account
- Authorize Tutorwise access
- Return to complete connection

### Step 3: Provide Business Information

**Individual Account:**
- Legal name
- Date of birth
- Home address
- Tax ID / National Insurance number

**Company Account:**
- Business name and type
- Business address
- Tax ID / Company number
- Representative information

### Step 4: Add Bank Details

**Required Information:**
- Bank name
- Account number
- Sort code (UK) or routing number (US)
- Account holder name

**Verification:**
- Stripe may make small test deposits
- Verify amounts in 1-2 business days
- Confirm in Stripe dashboard

### Step 5: Upload ID

**Accepted Documents:**
- Passport
- Driver's license
- National ID card

**Requirements:**
- Clear, readable image
- All four corners visible
- Not expired
- Matches your legal name

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Photography Tip**: Use good lighting and a plain background. Ensure all text is clearly legible.
</CalloutBox>

### Step 6: Review and Submit

Check all information is correct:
- Personal/business details
- Bank account
- Tax information
- ID documents

Click "Submit for Review"

## Verification Timeline

**Instant Approval** (most cases):
- Basic details verified immediately
- Can start receiving payments right away
- Full verification completes in background

**Manual Review** (some cases):
- Takes 1-3 business days
- Stripe may request additional information
- You'll receive email updates

**Status Check:**
- View in Account → Professional Info
- Stripe dashboard: dashboard.stripe.com
- Email notifications for status changes

## Common Verification Issues

**ID Rejected?**
- Ensure photo is clear and complete
- Check ID hasn't expired
- Try different document type
- Contact support if persistent issues

**Bank Details Incorrect?**
- Double-check account number and sort code
- Ensure account is in your name
- Some accounts (savings, business) may require additional verification

**Address Mismatch?**
- Address must match ID and bank records
- Provide proof of address if requested
- Update ID if recently moved

## After Approval

**Connection Confirmed:**
- Green checkmark in Account settings
- "Connected to Stripe" status
- Ready to receive payments

**Test Your Setup:**
- Complete a test booking (if possible)
- Check payout schedule
- Verify bank connection

## Managing Your Stripe Account

### Viewing Payouts

**In Tutorwise:**
- Go to Financials → Payouts
- See pending and completed payouts
- Download statements

**In Stripe Dashboard:**
- Visit dashboard.stripe.com
- View detailed transaction history
- Manage bank accounts
- Update business information

### Payout Schedule

**Default Schedule:**
- Weekly automatic transfers
- Paid every Friday
- Minimum payout: £10

**How It Works:**
1. Student completes session Monday
2. Payment processes by Tuesday
3. Added to your payout batch
4. Transferred Friday
5. In your bank Monday-Wednesday

### Updating Bank Details

1. Log in to dashboard.stripe.com
2. Go to Settings → Bank Accounts
3. Add new bank account
4. Verify with test deposits
5. Set as default
6. Remove old account (optional)

## Troubleshooting

**Can't connect Stripe?**
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Disable ad blockers
- Check Stripe is available in your country

**Payouts not arriving?**
- Check bank details are correct
- Verify minimum payout reached (£10)
- Allow 2-5 business days
- Contact support if delayed

**Account suspended?**
- Review Stripe's email for reason
- Provide requested information
- Contact Stripe support directly
- May take 5-7 days to resolve
