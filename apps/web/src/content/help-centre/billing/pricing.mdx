<CalloutBox type="info">
**For Students**: No platform fees. You pay exactly what the tutor charges.
**For Tutors**: 15% platform fee on bookings.
</CalloutBox>

## For Students: What You Pay

### No Hidden Fees

**You Pay Only:**
- Tutor's hourly rate
- Session duration
- No booking fees
- No platform fees
- No service charges

**Example:**
```
Tutor Rate: £40/hour
Session: 1 hour
You Pay: £40
```

### Payment Processing

**When Charged:**
- After session completes
- Within 24 hours of completion
- Never charged before session

**Refund Policy:**
- Cancel more than 24 hours: Full refund
- Cancel less than 24 hours: 50% refund
- Tutor cancels: Full refund always

## For Tutors: Platform Fees

### Standard Commission: 15%

**What You Receive:**
```
Session Price:      £100
Platform Fee (15%): -£15
You Receive:         £85
```

**Fee Covers:**
- Payment processing
- Platform maintenance
- Student acquisition
- Customer support
- Fraud protection
- Mobile apps

### When Fees Apply

**Charged On:**
- Completed bookings only
- Recurring sessions
- Package deals

**Not Charged On:**
- Cancelled sessions (tutor)
- No-shows (student)
- Declined bookings

## Setting Your Rates

### Pricing Guidelines

**Consider:**
- Your qualifications and experience
- Subject and level (GCSE, A-Level, University)
- Market rates in your area
- Online vs in-person
- Session duration options

**Typical UK Rates:**
- Primary: £15-25/hour
- GCSE: £20-35/hour
- A-Level: £30-50/hour
- University: £40-80/hour

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Tip**: Research similar tutors on the Marketplace to set competitive rates.
</CalloutBox>

### Package Discounts

Encourage recurring bookings with package deals:

**Suggested Discounts:**
- 5 sessions: 5% off
- 10 sessions: 10% off
- 20 or more sessions: 15% off

**Example:**
- Regular: £40/hour × 10 = £400
- Package: £36/hour × 10 = £360
- Student saves £40, you secure £360 upfront

## Refunds and Cancellations

### Student Refunds

**Cancellation Policy:**
- More than 24 hours: 100% refund
- Less than 24 hours: 50% refund
- No-show: No refund

**Processing Time:**
- Instant for cancellations
- 3-5 business days back to card
- Automatic, no action required

### Tutor Compensation

**If Student Cancels:**
- More than 24 hours: £0 (full refund to student)
- Less than 24 hours: 50% of session price
- No-show: 100% of session price

## Tax Implications

**Tax Responsibility**: Tutors are responsible for declaring and paying taxes on income earned.

- Declare on tax return
- Keep earnings records
