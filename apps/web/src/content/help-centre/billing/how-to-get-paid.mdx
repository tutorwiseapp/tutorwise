## Payment Flow Overview

### The Complete Journey

1. **Student Books Session**: Payment method authorized, not charged
2. **Session Completes**: Both parties confirm completion
3. **Payment Processes**: Student charged within 24 hours
4. **Platform Fee Deducted**: 10% commission
5. **Added to Payout**: Included in next weekly batch or manual withdrawal
6. **Bank Transfer**: Automated Friday payouts or on-demand
7. **Money in Bank**: Arrives 2-5 business days later

## Understanding Platform Fees

### Standard Commission Structure

**Platform Fee: 10%** (Updated Feb 2026)

**What This Covers:**
- Payment processing (Stripe fees)
- Platform development and maintenance
- Customer support

**Example Calculation:**
```
Session Price:        £100.00
Platform Fee (10%):   -£10.00
Your Earnings:         £90.00
```

If client was referred by an agent:
```

Referral Commission:  -£10.00 (paid to referring agent)
Your Earnings:         £80.00
```

## Payout Schedule

### Two Withdrawal Options (New!)

You now have flexibility in how you receive payments:

#### Option 1: Weekly Automatic Payouts
**Payout Day**: Every Friday at 10:00 AM UTC
**Cutoff Time**: Thursday 11:59 PM
**Minimum Amount**: £20 for teaching earnings, £25 for referral commissions

**How It Works:**
- All cleared transactions automatically batched
- Transferred to Stripe Connect account
- Arrives in bank 2-5 business days later
- No action needed from you
- Track in Financials dashboard

#### Option 2: Manual Withdrawals (New!)
**Anytime**: Request withdrawal on-demand
**Processing**: 2-3 business days to your bank
**Requirements**: Available balance only (not clearing funds)

**How to Request:**
1. Go to [Financials](/financials)
2. Click "Request Withdrawal" button
3. Review available balance and terms
4. Confirm withdrawal
5. Email confirmation sent
6. Funds transferred to Stripe Connect account

**Limitations:**
- One active withdrawal at a time
- 15-minute cooldown between requests
- Cannot withdraw clearing funds (must wait 7 days)
- Must have connected Stripe account

**Withdrawal History:**
- New "Withdrawal History" section on Financials page
- Track all past withdrawals
- See status: Processing, Completed, Failed
- Link to Stripe Dashboard for each payout

<CalloutBox type="success">
**Best Practice:** Use automatic payouts for consistent cash flow, or manual withdrawals when you need money immediately. Both options are free with no additional fees!
</CalloutBox>

### Minimum Payout Threshold

**£10 Minimum:**
- Earnings must reach £10 to trigger payout
- Balance carries over to next week if under £10
- No maximum payout limit
- No fees for payouts

**Example:**
- Week 1: Earn £8 (carried over)
- Week 2: Earn £7 (total £15)
- Payout: £15 transferred Friday

## Tax and Documentation

### Tax Responsibility

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Important**: You are responsible for paying taxes on your tutoring income. Tutorwise does not withhold taxes.
</CalloutBox>

**Self-Employment:**
- Tutoring income is self-employment income
- You must declare it on your tax return
- Keep records of all earnings
- Consider quarterly estimated tax payments

**UK Tutors:**
- Register for Self Assessment if needed
- Declare tutoring income on tax return
- Keep records for 5 years
- Consider hiring an accountant

**VAT Registration:**
- Required if turnover exceeds £85,000/year
- Voluntary registration available
- Adds complexity to bookkeeping

## Payment Methods

### How Funds Are Transferred

**Via Stripe:**
- All payouts processed through Stripe
- Must have connected Stripe account
- Funds transferred to your bank via Stripe

**Bank Transfer:**
- Standard ACH/BACS transfer
- No fees for standard transfer
- 2-5 business days
- Faster Payments (UK) where available

### Updating Bank Details

**To Change Bank Account:**
1. Log in to Stripe Dashboard
2. Go to Settings → Bank Accounts
3. Add new bank account
4. Verify with micro-deposits
5. Set as default
6. Previous account auto-removed after verification

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Tip**: Keep old account active until first payout to new account succeeds.
</CalloutBox>

## Common Payment Issues

### Delayed Payouts

**Reasons for Delays:**
- Bank holidays (payouts pushed to next business day)
- Stripe verification pending
- Bank account details incorrect
- Minimum threshold not reached

**What to Do:**
- Check Financials dashboard for payout status
- Verify bank details in Stripe
- Contact support if delayed more than 7 days

### Missing Payments

**Session Not Showing:**
- Allow 24 hours for payment to process
- Check session was marked complete by both parties
- Verify student's payment method didn't decline
- Contact support with session ID

**Payout Not Received:**
- Check Stripe dashboard for payout status
- Verify bank account is correct
- Allow full 5 business days
- Contact bank if Stripe shows "paid"

### Disputed Payments

**Student Disputes Charge:**
You'll see a prominent **red "Payment Disputed" banner** on the transaction:
- Stripe handles dispute process automatically
- Funds held until investigation completes
- You have 7 days to respond with evidence
- Click "View in Stripe Dashboard" to upload proof

**What to Upload:**
- Session recordings or screenshots
- Message history with student
- Attendance records
- Work completed/materials provided
- Any other proof of service delivery

**Your Protection:**
- Keep detailed session notes in WiseSpace
- Save all student communications
- Document attendance in real-time
- Respond within 7 days for best results
- Contact support@tutorwise.com if you need help

**Dispute Banner:**
The new dispute alert shows:
- Clear red warning at top of transaction
- Dispute reason and status
- Direct link to Stripe Dashboard
- Instructions for evidence submission
- Timeline for response

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Act Fast:** If you see a red dispute banner, respond within 7 days. Late responses significantly reduce your chances of winning the dispute.
</CalloutBox>
