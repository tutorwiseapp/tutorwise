# Profile Setup Guide

Your profile is your first impression on Tutorwise. A complete, professional profile builds trust and attracts more bookings.

<CalloutBox type="info">
**Complete profiles** get 3x more bookings than incomplete ones. Take 15 minutes to set yours up properly.
</CalloutBox>

## Profile Essentials

### Required Information

**All Users:**
- Full name
- Profile photo
- Email address (verified)
- Account type (Tutor/Student/Agent)

**Tutors Additionally:**
- Bio (200-500 words)
- Qualifications
- Teaching experience
- Subjects and levels
- Availability

## Profile Photo

### Photo Guidelines

**Best Practices:**
- Professional headshot
- Smiling, friendly expression
- Good lighting
- Plain or blurred background
- Face clearly visible
- Recent photo (within 2 years)

**Technical Requirements:**
- Minimum 400×400 pixels
- Maximum 5MB file size
- Formats: JPG, PNG
- Square or portrait orientation

<CalloutBox type="warning">
**Avoid**: Selfies, group photos, sunglasses, heavily filtered images, or photos with text overlays.
</CalloutBox>

### Why Photos Matter

Profiles with photos:
- Get 5x more views
- Receive 3x more booking requests
- Build immediate trust
- Appear more professional

## Writing Your Bio

### Bio Structure

**Paragraph 1: Hook (2-3 sentences)**
- Your strongest credential
- Years of experience
- Notable achievement

**Example:**
> "Hi, I'm Sarah! I'm a qualified teacher with 10 years of experience teaching GCSE Mathematics. My students consistently achieve A and A* grades, with a 95% pass rate."

**Paragraph 2: Teaching Approach (3-4 sentences)**
- Your methodology
- What makes you different
- How you help students

**Example:**
> "I believe every student can excel in math with the right approach. I use real-world examples and visual aids to make complex concepts understandable. My sessions are patient, engaging, and tailored to your learning style."

**Paragraph 3: What Students Get (2-3 sentences)**
- Session structure
- Materials provided
- Support offered

**Example:**
> "Each session includes personalized lesson plans and practice worksheets. I provide homework after sessions and offer email support between lessons for quick questions."

### Bio Tips

**Do:**
- Write in first person ("I" not "She/He")
- Use "you" to speak directly to students
- Be specific about results
- Show personality
- Keep it under 500 words

**Don't:**
- Use jargon or complex language
- Make unsupported claims
- Copy other tutors' bios
- Include contact information
- Write in third person

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Tip**: Read your bio aloud. If it sounds natural and engaging, it's good!
</CalloutBox>

## Adding Qualifications

### Education

**Include:**
- Degrees (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD)
- Teaching certifications (PGCE, QTS)
- Subject-specific qualifications
- University names and graduation years

**Example:**
```
BSc Mathematics, University of Manchester (2015)
PGCE Secondary Mathematics, UCL (2016)
A-Level: Maths (A*), Further Maths (A*), Physics (A)
```

### Teaching Experience

**Details to Add:**
- Years teaching
- Age groups taught
- Institutions (schools, tutoring centers)
- Specializations

**Example:**
```
Mathematics Teacher, Manchester Grammar School (2016-2020)
Private Tutor, Self-employed (2020-Present)
Specialization: GCSE and A-Level exam preparation
300+ students taught, 95% achieved A-A* grades
```

### Certifications

**Relevant Certifications:**
- DBS check (UK)
- First Aid
- Safeguarding training
- Subject-specific certifications

**Upload:**
- Clear scans or photos
- All four corners visible
- Text readable
- Not expired

## Setting Availability

### Calendar Best Practices

**Regular Schedule:**
- Set consistent weekly hours
- Include peak times (evenings, weekends)
- Allow 15-30 minute buffers between sessions
- Block personal commitments

**Optimal Availability:**
- Weekday evenings: 4-8 PM
- Saturdays: 9 AM-5 PM
- Sundays: 10 AM-4 PM
- Minimum 10-15 hours/week for regular bookings

<CalloutBox type="success">
**Success Tip**: Tutors with 15+ hours weekly availability get 4x more bookings.
</CalloutBox>

## Verification

### Identity Verification

**Why Verify:**
- Builds trust with students
- Increases booking rate
- Required for certain features
- Shows on profile badge

**How to Verify:**
1. Go to Account → Settings
2. Click "Verify Identity"
3. Upload government ID
4. Take selfie for comparison
5. Wait 24-48 hours for approval

**Accepted IDs:**
- Passport
- Driver's license
- National ID card

### DBS Check (UK Tutors)

**Enhanced DBS:**
- Required for under-18s
- Shows on profile
- Increases parent trust
- Upload certificate copy

**How to Get:**
- Apply through DBS Update Service
- Upload to Tutorwise
- Renewal every 3 years

## Video Introduction (Optional)

### Why Add Video?

**Benefits:**
- 3x more likely to get bookings
- Shows personality
- Builds immediate connection
- Demonstrates teaching style

### Video Guidelines

**Content (30-60 seconds):**
- Introduce yourself
- Explain your teaching approach
- Show enthusiasm
- Speak directly to camera

**Technical:**
- Good lighting (natural or ring light)
- Clear audio
- Plain background
- Horizontal orientation
- Maximum 2 minutes

<CalloutBox type="tip">
**Script Template**: "Hi, I'm [Name]! I teach [Subject] to [Level] students. My approach focuses on [Teaching Style]. I love helping students [Achievement]. Book a session to see how I can help you succeed!"
</CalloutBox>

## Privacy Settings

### What's Public?

**Always Visible:**
- Name and photo
- Bio
- Qualifications
- Reviews
- Listings

**Never Visible:**
- Email address
- Phone number
- Full address
- Bank details
- Payment history

### Control Your Visibility

**Profile Settings:**
- Show/hide last active
- Display/hide age
- Public or private profile
- Search visibility

## Updating Your Profile

### Keep It Fresh

**Regular Updates:**
- Add new qualifications
- Update availability seasonally
- Refresh bio annually
- Add new teaching experience
- Update photo every 2-3 years

**Seasonal Adjustments:**
- Exam season: Highlight exam prep
- Summer: Offer catch-up sessions
- September: Focus on new academic year
