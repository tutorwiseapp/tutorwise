# Getting Started as a Tutor

Welcome to Tutorwise! This guide will help you set up your profile and start accepting bookings.

## 1. Complete Your Profile

Your profile is your first impression to potential students. Make it count!

### Essential Information
- **Professional Photo**: Upload a clear, professional headshot
- **Bio**: Write a compelling 2-3 paragraph introduction
- **Qualifications**: Add your degrees, certifications, and credentials
- **Teaching Experience**: Highlight your expertise and years of experience

### Pro Tips
- Use a friendly, approachable tone in your bio
- Mention specific subjects you excel at
- Include any notable achievements or specializations

## 2. Create Your First Listing

Listings showcase the subjects and services you offer.

### Steps to Create a Listing
1. Navigate to **Listings** from the sidebar
2. Click **Create Listing**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Subject**: What you teach (e.g., "GCSE Mathematics")
   - **Level**: Student level (Primary, GCSE, A-Level, University)
   - **Hourly Rate**: Your pricing (we recommend Â£25-45/hour)
   - **Description**: What makes your teaching unique
   - **Availability**: Your typical weekly schedule

### Pricing Strategy
- Research similar tutors in your area
- Consider your experience level
- Start competitive, adjust based on demand

## 3. Set Your Availability

Students can only book when you're available.

1. Go to **Account > Settings**
2. Click **Availability**
3. Set your recurring weekly schedule
4. Mark specific dates as unavailable (holidays, etc.)

## 4. Manage Bookings

When students book sessions with you:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Review the booking in **Bookings** page
3. Accept or decline within 24 hours
4. Add the session to your calendar

## 5. Get Paid

### How Payments Work
- Students pay upfront when booking
- Funds are held securely by Tutorwise
- You receive payment 24 hours after the session
- Payments go to your connected Stripe account

### Setting Up Payments
1. Go to **Payments**
2. Click **Connect Stripe Account**
3. Complete the Stripe onboarding
4. Add your bank details

## 6. Build Your Reputation

Reviews are crucial for attracting more students.

### Earning Great Reviews
- Be punctual and prepared
- Communicate clearly with students
- Follow up after sessions
- Ask satisfied students to leave reviews
