# Cancellation & Refund Policy

Life happens. We understand that sometimes you need to cancel a session. Here's how our cancellation policy works.

## Quick Summary

**For Clients:**
- Cancel 24+ hours before: **100% refund**
- Cancel less than 24 hours: **No refund**
- No-show: **No refund**

**For Tutors:**
- Cancel anytime: Client gets **100% refund** + your reputation is affected
- No-show: Client gets **100% refund** + major reputation penalty

---

## Client Cancellations

### 24+ Hours Notice (Full Refund)

If you cancel **24 hours or more** before your session:
- ‚úÖ **Full refund** to your original payment method (minus Stripe processing fee)
- ‚úÖ Refund processed within 5-10 business days
- ‚úÖ No questions asked
- ‚úÖ No impact on your account

**About Stripe Processing Fees:**
When you pay for a session, Stripe (our payment processor) charges a small fee (1.5% + 20p for UK/EU cards). When we refund you, Stripe keeps this fee. This means if you paid ¬£50, you'll receive approximately ¬£49.05 back.

**How to Cancel:**
1. Go to **Bookings** in your dashboard
2. Find your upcoming session
3. Click **Cancel Booking**
4. Confirm cancellation
5. You'll receive a confirmation email with refund details

### Less Than 24 Hours Notice (No Refund)

If you cancel **less than 24 hours** before your session:
- ‚ùå **No refund** - tutor receives full payment

**Why?** Tutors rely on advance notice to fill their slots. Last-minute cancellations mean they lose income with no time to find replacement students.

### Client No-Show

- ‚ùå **No refund** - tutor receives full payment

## Refund Process

### How Refunds Work

1. **Automatic Processing:** Refunds are processed automatically when you cancel with 24+ hours notice or report a tutor no-show
2. **Refund Amount:** You receive the full amount **minus Stripe's processing fee** (1.5% + 20p for UK/EU cards)
3. **Refund Timeline:** Money returns to your original payment method within 5-10 business days
4. **Confirmation Email:** You'll receive an email confirming your refund with expected timeline

### Refund Methods

- **Credit/Debit Card:** 5-7 business days
- **PayPal:** 3-5 business days
- **Bank Transfer:** 7-10 business days

**Note:** Refund timing depends on your bank's processing speed, not TutorWise.

### Why is the Stripe Fee Deducted?

When you make a payment, Stripe (our secure payment processor) charges a transaction fee. This fee is non-refundable‚Äîeven when we issue a refund, Stripe keeps their processing fee. This is standard practice across all platforms using Stripe and protects the platform from absorbing these costs.

## Emergency Exceptions

### Medical Emergencies

If you have a medical emergency that prevents you from attending:
1. Contact **support@tutorwise.com** within 24 hours
2. Provide medical documentation (doctor's note, hospital discharge, etc.)
3. Our team will review and may issue an exception refund

### Other Exceptional Circumstances

We handle these on a case-by-case basis:
- Family emergencies (with proof)
- Extreme weather preventing attendance
- Exam cancellations (with proof)
- Platform technical issues

**To Request Exception:** Email **support@tutorwise.com** with:
- Booking ID
- Reason for cancellation
- Supporting documentation
- Response within 48 hours

## Rescheduling vs Cancelling

### Rescheduling (Recommended)

Instead of cancelling, consider rescheduling:
- ‚úÖ No refund needed - payment transfers to new date
- ‚úÖ Keep your tutor (no need to rebook)
- ‚úÖ No impact on either party

**How to Reschedule:**
1. Message your tutor in the booking chat
2. Agree on a new time
3. Click **Reschedule** in booking details
4. Confirm new time
5. Both parties receive confirmation

**Limit:** You can reschedule up to 4 times per booking. After that, you'll need to cancel and create a new booking.

## What Counts as "Session Start Time"?

The 24-hour cancellation window is based on your **confirmed session start time**.

**Example:**
- Session scheduled: Thursday 3:00 PM
- 24-hour cutoff: Wednesday 3:00 PM
- Cancel Wednesday 2:00 PM = Full refund ‚úÖ
- Cancel Wednesday 4:00 PM = No refund ‚ùå

**Timezone:** All times are based on the timezone specified when booking was created (usually tutor's timezone).

## FAQs

### Can I cancel if my tutor is late?

If your tutor is **15+ minutes late** without notice:
- Wait 15 minutes past session start
- Try to contact them via platform messages
- If no response, report as no-show for automatic refund

### What if I have a technical issue?

If platform technical issues prevent your session:
- Contact support immediately during the session
- We'll investigate and issue full refund if confirmed
- Both parties protected - no penalty

### Can I cancel a recurring booking?

Yes, but:
- Each session follows the same 24-hour policy
- Cancel early sessions with 24+ hours notice for refund
- Later sessions can be cancelled individually

### What if my tutor cancels repeatedly?

- First cancellation: Full refund + 10 CaaS penalty (tutor)
- Repeated cancellations: Progressive CaaS penalties
- Consistent cancellers: Account review/suspension
- You're always protected with automatic refunds

## CaaS (Credibility as a Service) Explained

### What is CaaS?

CaaS is our tutor reputation system. Think of it like a credit score for tutors.

- High CaaS (100+): Top search ranking, more bookings
- Medium CaaS (50-100): Normal search ranking
- Low CaaS (0-50): Hidden from main search
- Negative CaaS (<0): Account suspended

### How CaaS is Affected

**Positive Actions:**
- Complete a session: +5 points
- Receive 5-star review: +10 points
- Perfect attendance (30 days): +25 points

**Negative Actions:**
- Cancel session: **-10 points**
- No-show: **-50 points**
- Client complaint: -20 points

**Recovery:** Tutors can rebuild CaaS through consistent delivery. 10 successful sessions = +50 points.
