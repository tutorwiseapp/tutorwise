# Organisation Feature - AI Prompt

**Version**: v6.0
**Date**: 2025-12-12

## Feature Overview

Multi-tenant organization management for agencies and schools to manage tutors and clients.

**Key Responsibilities**:
- Organization creation and management
- Member invitations and management
- Role-based access control
- Unified billing and metrics

## Database Tables

- `organizations` - Organization profiles
- `organization_members` - Member associations
- `organization_invitations` - Pending invitations
